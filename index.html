About Me

Hi - I'm Isaac, a self-taught front end web developer currently residing in New Zealand.

I began my web development journey in June 2020 after discovering a passion for bringing ideas to life via code. While juggling a full-time digital marketing job, I decided to invest in myself and learn to code through the Front-End Web Development TechDegree with Treehouse.

I love learning new things and enjoy the challenge of creating websites. I thoroughly enjoy the process of turning ideas into designs and then turning designs into reality.

In my spare time, you'll often find me reading books, playing video games or enjoying the many sporting options New Zealand has to offer.
